# 🎣 Phishing Attack - My Notes

## What is Phishing?
Phishing is a fake email/message to steal user data like password, bank info.

## Types of Phishing I Learned (TryHackMe)
1. **Email Phishing** - Fake email from bank/company
2. **Spear Phishing** - Targeted attack on specific person
3. **Whaling** - Target CEO/Boss
4. **Smishing** - Phishing via SMS
5. **Vishing** - Phishing via Voice Call

## How to Identify Phishing Email?
- Check sender email (spelling mistake)
- Urgent language - "Your account will be closed!"
- Suspicious link - hover to see real URL
- Ask for password - real company never asks

## How to Stay Safe?
- Never click unknown links
- Enable 2FA
- Verify sender
- Use antivirus
